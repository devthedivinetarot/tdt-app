## Information we collect

**Account information.** When you sign in, we collect:
- **Google sign-in:** your name, email address, and Google account identifier.
- **Phone sign-in:** your mobile phone number (verified by SMS one-time password).

**Profile information you provide.** Name, gender, birth place, date of birth, time
of birth, marital status, occupation, and a profile photo you choose to add. You
enter this to personalise readings and consultations; you can edit or clear it.

**Reading activity.** Your tarot questions and drawn cards. Your reading history is
stored **on your device**.

**Device & session information (analytics).** When you open the App we may record:
timestamp, an app-generated install identifier, platform (Android/iOS/web), device
brand, manufacturer and model, operating system and version, and the App version.
This helps us understand usage and improve the App. You can turn this off (see
"Your choices").

**Location (optional, off by default).** Only if you enable "Include my location"
in Profile → Privacy **and** grant the device location permission, we collect your
approximate location (city, region, country, and coordinates) to understand which
regions our users come from. You can turn this off at any time.

**Payment information.** Payments are processed by **Razorpay**. We do **not**
collect or store your card, UPI, or bank details — Razorpay handles them directly.
We may receive and store payment metadata such as an order ID, payment ID, amount,
and status to confirm your purchase and unlock features.

## How we use information
- To provide the reading, Kundli, and course features.
- To create and manage your account and sign you in.
- To personalise readings and pre-fill consultation details.
- To process payments and unlock paid features.
- To send local reminder notifications (if you enable them).
- To understand usage, fix bugs, and improve the App.
- To comply with legal obligations.

## How information is shared
We do not sell your personal information. We share it only with service providers
that help us run the App, and only as needed:

- **Google Firebase** (Authentication, Firestore database) — stores your account and
  profile so you can sign in across devices.
- **Razorpay** — processes payments. See Razorpay's privacy policy at
  razorpay.com/privacy.
- **Google Sheets / Google Apps Script** — receives the device/session (and, if you
  enabled it, location) records and payment confirmations described above.
- **Teachable** — if you enrol in a course, your enrolment details are handled by
  Teachable under its own privacy policy.

We may also disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights.

## Data retention
- Account and profile data: kept until you ask us to delete it or delete your account.
- Reading history: stored on your device; removed when you clear it or uninstall.
- Analytics and payment logs: retained as long as needed for the purposes above.

## Your choices and rights
- **Analytics opt-out:** Profile → Privacy → turn off "Share usage & device info".
- **Location:** Profile → Privacy → turn "Include my location" on/off; you can also
  revoke the permission in your device settings.
- **Access, correction, deletion:** email **[contact email]** to request a copy of
  your data, correct it, or delete your account and associated data.
- **Notifications:** disable reminders in the App or your device settings.

Depending on where you live, you may have rights under laws such as India's Digital
Personal Data Protection Act, the EU/UK GDPR, or the California CCPA. We honour these
rights; contact us to exercise them.

## Permissions the App may request
- **Location** — only if you enable location logging (optional).
- **Photos / media** — to set a profile photo you choose.
- **Notifications** — to send reading reminders you opt into.
- **Internet** — to sign in, run readings, sync your profile, and process payments.

## Security
We use reputable providers (Google Firebase, Razorpay) and reasonable safeguards to
protect your information. Your payment card details are handled by Razorpay and never
stored by us. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

## Children's privacy
The App is intended for adults and is **not directed to children under 18**. We do
not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided us
data, contact us and we will delete it.

## International users
Your information may be processed on servers operated by our providers (e.g. Google,
Razorpay) in countries other than yours. By using the App you consent to this
processing.

## Changes to this policy
